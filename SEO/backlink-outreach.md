## Outreach Campaign Plan

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-2)
- Set up all directory listings (local and legal)
- Claim and optimize Google Business Profile
- Submit to government resource pages
- Create 3 linkable assets (guides/infographics)

**Target**: 20-30 foundational links

---

### Phase 2: Content Outreach (Month 3-4)
- Pitch 10 guest post ideas to legal publications
- Submit 5 expert commentary pitches to business media
- Reach out to 15 educational institutions with resources
- Create 2 more linkable assets

**Target**: 5-10 high-quality editorial links

---

### Phase 3: Relationship Building (Month 5-6)
- Follow up on pending outreach
- Conduct 2-3 webinars with partner institutions
- Publish case studies and promote to industry media
- Start broken link building campaign (identify 50 opportunities)

**Target**: 8-12 quality links + partnerships

---

### Phase 4: Scale & Optimize (Month 7-12)
- Continue guest posting (1-2 per month)
- Leverage existing relationships for more opportunities
- Create and promote annual report/research
- Monitor and replicate successful tactics

**Target**: 15-25 quality links

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do:
✅ Personalize every outreach email
✅ Provide value first, ask for link second
✅ Follow up (but not more than 2 times)
✅ Build genuine relationships
✅ Create truly helpful content
✅ Track all outreach systematically
✅ Focus on relevance over quantity

### Don't:
❌ Buy links or use link schemes
❌ Use automated spam tools
❌ Send generic templated emails without personalization
❌ Ask for links without offering value
❌ Target low-quality or spammy sites
❌ Over-optimize anchor text
❌ Give up after one rejection
